Our Story

The Tradition Behind the Fire

Born from a Love of Fire & Family

J.A.M.'s Brazilian Steakhouse was founded by Jack A. Mullins, who grew up eating churrasco at family gatherings and always dreamed of bringing that tradition to Oxford, Ohio. What started as backyard cookouts turned into a full-blown obsession with fire, flavor, and feeding people until they can't move.

In 2024, Jack opened the doors of J.A.M.'s — a place where you sit down, relax, and let our gauchos take care of everything. No rush. No limits. Just good meat, good company, and the kind of meal you remember for years.

The name J.A.M. isn't just initials — it's a promise that every visit will be packed with flavor, warmth, and that jam-packed feeling of being completely satisfied.

What We Stand For

🥩

Quality First

We source our beef from family-owned farms that prioritize humane raising and natural feeding. Every cut is hand-selected and seasoned simply — because great meat doesn't need to hide behind heavy sauces.

🤝

Genuine Hospitality

We're not a chain. We're a family restaurant run by people who genuinely love feeding guests. Our servers know your name by the end of the night, and our gauchos take pride in every slice they serve.

🌿

Fresh Every Day

Our salad bar is restocked throughout service. Our sauces are made in-house daily. Nothing sits around. If it's on your plate, it was prepared today — that's non-negotiable at J.A.M.'s.
